Biography

Albert Einstein .The special and general theories of relativity were created by German mathematician and physicist Albert Einstein. He received the Physics Nobel Prize in 1921 for his analysis of the photoelectric phenomenon. After being targeted by the German Nazi Party, he immigrated to the United States in the ensuing ten years. His work had a significant influence on the advancement of atomic energy as well.
